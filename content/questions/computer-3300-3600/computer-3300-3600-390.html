yum remove PackageKit-command-not-found\n

jika Anda mendapatkan kesalahan, 

bash: /usr/libexec/pk-command-not-found: No such file or directory\n

Anda perlu menambahkan baris berikut ke ~ / .bashrc 

unset command_not_found_handle\n
