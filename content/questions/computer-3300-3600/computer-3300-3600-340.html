Saya ingin memindahkan kata "H1" ke akhir baris

\n\n
    \n

  • Menu "Search"> "Replace" (atau Ctrl + H )

    • \n

  • Setel "Temukan apa" ke ^(.*)\\bH1\\b(.*)$.

    • \n

  • Setel "Replace with" ke \\1\\2H1

    • \n

  • Aktifkan "Ekspresi reguler"

    • \n

  • Klik "Ganti Semua"

    \n\n

    • \n
\n\n

Sebelum: 

Kuu(ij,mn)=-H1/A1/AA2*R0(ij,mn)*DA1Y*DQ66Y+...\nH1/A1/AAA2*R0(ij,mn)*Q66*DA1Y*DA2Y-...\n1/H1/AA1/AA2*H2*R0(ij,mn)*Q66*DA1Y^2+...\n1/AA1/H1*DH2X*Q11*RX(ij,mn)-...\n1/AAA1*H2/H1*Q11*RX(ij,mn)*DA1X+...\n1/H1/AA1/A2*H2*Q11*RX(ij,mn)*DA2X;\n

Setelah: 

Kuu(ij,mn)=-/A1/AA2*R0(ij,mn)*DA1Y*DQ66Y+...H1\n/A1/AAA2*R0(ij,mn)*Q66*DA1Y*DA2Y-...H1\n1//AA1/AA2*H2*R0(ij,mn)*Q66*DA1Y^2+...H1\n1/AA1/*DH2X*Q11*RX(ij,mn)-...H1\n1/AAA1*H2/*Q11*RX(ij,mn)*DA1X+...H1\n1//AA1/A2*H2*Q11*RX(ij,mn)*DA2X;H1\n
\n\n
