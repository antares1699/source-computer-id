Jika Anda ingin menghapus OneDrive secara permanen , lakukan hal berikut:

\n\n

Buat file teks dengan membuka Notepad

\n\n

Pergilah link berikut dan salin / tempel kode ke dalam file teks: 

@echo off\ncls\n\nset x86=\"%SYSTEMROOT%\\System32\\OneDriveSetup.exe\"\nset x64=\"%SYSTEMROOT%\\SysWOW64\\OneDriveSetup.exe\"\n\necho Closing OneDrive process.\necho.\ntaskkill /f /im OneDrive.exe > NUL 2>&1\nping 127.0.0.1 -n 5 > NUL 2>&1\n\necho Uninstalling OneDrive.\necho.\nif exist %x64% (\n%x64% /uninstall\n) else (\n%x86% /uninstall\n)\nping 127.0.0.1 -n 5 > NUL 2>&1\n\necho Removing OneDrive leftovers.\necho.\nrd \"%USERPROFILE%\\OneDrive\" /Q /S > NUL 2>&1\nrd \"C:\\OneDriveTemp\" /Q /S > NUL 2>&1\nrd \"%LOCALAPPDATA%\\Microsoft\\OneDrive\" /Q /S > NUL 2>&1\nrd \"%PROGRAMDATA%\\Microsoft OneDrive\" /Q /S > NUL 2>&1 \n\necho Removeing OneDrive from the Explorer Side Panel.\necho.\nREG DELETE \"HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\\CLSID\\{018D5C66-4533-4307-9B53-224DE2ED1FE6}\" /f > NUL 2>&1\nREG DELETE \"HKEY_CLASSES_ROOT\\Wow6432Node\\CLSID\\{018D5C66-4533-4307-9B53-224DE2ED1FE6}\" /f > NUL 2>&1\n\npause\n

Kemudian simpan sebagai RemoveOneDrive.bat dan keluar

\n\n

Jalankan skrip dengan hak admin dan One Drive akan hilang
