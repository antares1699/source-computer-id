Saat menggunakan input berikut ke nsupdate untuk menetapkan record A dan record PTR secara bersamaan: 

server dns01.example.com\nupdate delete pup01.example.com. A\nupdate add pup01.example.com. 300 A 172.29.207.209\nupdate delete 209.207.29.172.in-addr.arpa. PTR\nupdate add 209.207.29.172.in-addr.arpa. 300 PTR pup01.example.com.\nsend\n

Kami mendapatkan kesalahan berikut yang dikembalikan oleh bind9: 

Sep 23 14:26:12 dns01 named[1070]: client 172.29.207.209#25781/key example.com: view example.com-default: updating zone 'example.com/IN': update failed: update RR is outside zone (NOTZONE)\n

Apa maksud dari kesalahan tersebut?

","source":"https://superuser.com/questions/977132/when-using-nsupdate-to-update-both-a-and-ptr-records-why-do-i-get-update-faile","author":"Graham Leggett","replies":[],"answers":[{"answer":"

Berdasarkan laporan bug dari 2004 di https://mail-index.netbsd.org/current-users/2004/06/19/0002.html , solusinya adalah sebagai berikut:

\n\n

Baris kosong perlu dimasukkan antara pengaturan rekam A dan pengaturan rekam PTR, seperti ini: 

server dns01.example.com\nupdate delete pup01.example.com. A\nupdate add pup01.example.com. 300 A 172.29.207.209\n[blank line goes here]\nupdate delete 209.207.29.172.in-addr.arpa. PTR\nupdate add 209.207.29.172.in-addr.arpa. 300 PTR pup01.example.com.\nsend\n

Ini menghasilkan yang berikut di log: 

Sep 23 14:40:12 dns01 named[1070]: client 172.29.207.209#36127/key example.com: view example.com-default: updating zone 'example.com/IN': deleting rrset at 'pup01.example.com' A\nSep 23 14:40:12 dns01 named[1070]: client 172.29.207.209#36127/key example.com: view example.com-default: updating zone 'example.com/IN': adding an RR at 'pup01.example.com' A\nSep 23 14:40:12 dns01 named[1070]: client 172.29.207.209#36127/key example.com: view example.com-default: updating zone '29.172.in-addr.arpa/IN': deleting rrset at '209.207.29.172.in-addr.arpa' PTR\nSep 23 14:40:12 dns01 named[1070]: client 172.29.207.209#36127/key example.com: view example.com-default: updating zone '29.172.in-addr.arpa/IN': adding an RR at '209.207.29.172.in-addr.arpa' PTR\n

Semoga ini menghemat waktu seseorang.
