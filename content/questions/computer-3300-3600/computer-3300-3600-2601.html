\n\n

\n\n

\n\n

\n\n

Keluaran: 

5: UTF-8\n6: VobSub SUBTiTLE: .............. French\n7: VobSub SUBTiTLE: .............. English\n8: VobSub SUBTiTLE: .............. French\n

Jérôme, pengembang MediaInfo
