\n\n

Jika direktori sumber yang Anda kompilasi tersedia, cari file config.nice, yang akan berisi daftar konfigurasi yang bagus. 

#! /bin/sh\n#\n# Created by configure\n\n\"./configure\" \\\n\"--prefix=/opt/apache\" \\\n\"--enable-rewrite\" \\\n\"--enable-so\" \\\n\"--enable-mime-magic\" \\\n\"--enable-cgi\" \\\n\"--enable-cache\" \\\n\"--enable-ssl\" \\\n\"--enable-proxy\" \\\n\"--enable-proxy-http\" \\\n\"--enable-proxy-balancer\" \\\n\"$@\"\n
