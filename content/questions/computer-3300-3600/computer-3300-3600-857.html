Gunakan pencarian paket untuk mencari paket dengan killall

\n\n

https://cygwin.com/cgi-bin2/package-grep.cgi

\n\n

https://cygwin.com/cgi-bin2/package-grep.cgi?grep=bin%2Fkillall&arch=x86_64

\n\n
\n

Hasil Pencarian Ditemukan 5 kecocokan untuk bin / killall 

busybox-1.23.2-1 - busybox: Tiny utilities in a single executable (installed binaries and support files)\npsmisc-debuginfo-22.20-1 - psmisc-debuginfo: Debug info for psmisc (installed binaries and support files)\npsmisc-22.20-1 - psmisc: Utilities for managing processes on your system (installed binaries and support files)\nsysvinit-debuginfo-2.86-1 - sysvinit-debuginfo: Debug info for sysvinit (installed binaries and support files)\nsysvinit-2.86-1 - sysvinit: A System-V Init Clone (installed binaries and support files)\n
\n\n

psmisc adalah yang Anda butuhkan.
