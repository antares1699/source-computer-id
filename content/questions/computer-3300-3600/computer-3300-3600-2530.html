  • Jika Folder Sumber , Folder tujuan , dan Daftar File berada di jalur yang sama:
    • \n
\n
FileList:          D:\\Files\\FileList.txt\nSourceFolder:      D:\\Files\\SourceFolder\nDestinationFolder: D:\\Files\\DestinationFolder\n

Dan FileList hanya berisi nama dan ekstensi file: 

File1.txt\nFile2.pdf\nFile3.docx\nFile4.jpg\n

Maka skrip Anda adalah: 

@echo off\npushd D:\\Files\nfor /f \"tokens=* delims=\" %%a in ('type FileList.txt') do xcopy /hrkvy \".\\SourceFolder\\%%a\" \".\\DestinationFolder\"\npopd\npause\n
\n
    \n
  • Jika Folder Sumber , Folder tujuan , dan Daftar File berada di jalur yang sama:
    • \n
\n
FileList:          D:\\Files\\FileList.txt\nSourceFolder:      D:\\Files\\SourceFolder\nDestinationFolder: D:\\Files\\DestinationFolder\n

Dan FileList berisi jalur lengkap ke file: 

D:\\Files\\SourceFolder\\File1.txt\nD:\\Files\\SourceFolder\\File2.jpg\nD:\\Files\\SourceFolder\\File3.docx\n

Maka skrip Anda adalah: 

@echo off\npushd D:\\Files\nfor /f \"tokens=* delims=\" %%a in ('type FileList.txt') do xcopy /hrkvy \"%%a\" \".\\DestinationFolder\"\npopd\npause\n
\n
    \n
  • Jika Folder tujuan , dan Daftar File berada di bawah jalur yang sama dan SourceFolder adalah variabel:
    • \n

File1.txt\nFile2.jpg\nFile3.docx\n

Maka skrip Anda adalah: 

@echo off\npushd D:\\Files\nset /p SourceFolder=Enter the path of source folder and press 'Enter':\ncls\nfor /f \"tokens=* delims=\" %%a in ('type FileList.txt') do xcopy /hrkvy \"%SourceFolder%\\%%a\" \".\\DestinationFolder\"\npopd\npause\n

@echo off\nfor /f \"tokens=* delims=\" %%a in ('type \"D:\\Files\\FileList.txt\"') do xcopy /hrkvy \"%%a\" \"C:\\DestinationFolder\"\npause\n

@echo off\npushd D:\\Missing Papers\nfor /f \"tokens=* delims=\" %%a in ('type MissingPapers.txt\"') do (\n for /f \"tokens=* delims=\" %%b in ('dir /b /s \"%OneDrive%\" ^| find /i \"%%a\"') do\n  xcopy /hrkvy \"%%b\" \".\\Searched out\"\n )\n)\npause\n
