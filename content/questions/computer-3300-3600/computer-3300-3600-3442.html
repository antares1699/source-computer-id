=IFERROR((MAX(C4:G4)-MIN(C4:G4))/(COUNTA(C4:G4)-1),\"\")\n

Jika Anda ingin melewati kolom pembantu: 

=IFERROR(MAX(C4:G4) + (MAX(C4:G4)-MIN(C4:G4))/(COUNTA(C4:G4)-1),\"\")\n

Anda juga dapat menggunakan FORCAST: 

=FORECAST(0,C4:G4,ROW($1:$5))\n

=INTERCEPT(C4:G4,ROW($1:$5))\n
